About Us

The 929 Fire Rescue Volunteer is a community-based emergency response group dedicated to fire suppression, rescue operations, disaster response, and public safety education. Our volunteers are trained to act quickly and professionally during emergencies.

Our Services

Fire Emergency Response

Immediate action during fire incidents to protect lives and property.

Rescue Operations

Helping victims during accidents, floods, and disasters.

Community Training

Fire safety seminars and emergency preparedness programs.

Radio Communication

Coordinated emergency communications for fast response.
